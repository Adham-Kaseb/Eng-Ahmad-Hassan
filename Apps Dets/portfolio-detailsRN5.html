    The Escavi brand was established in 1991 as a small store in Al Ain city in the United Arab Emirates. Our products were exclusively imported from Italy, known for its high-quality products. The durability of Escavi sandals in all weather conditions across the country is a unique feature. In 2007, Escavi Group imported a factory from Italy and installed it in Al Ain city in the UAE, with the goal of providing the same Italian reliability, focusing on quality, attention to detail, and luxury. Escavi Group quickly became a pioneer in the concept of custom sandals, allowing customers to create their own masterpiece, choosing from a variety of leathers and other parts. In addition to introducing a new concept, Escavi Group adheres to the principle of excellence in service at an affordable cost.
